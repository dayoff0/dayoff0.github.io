Basic omelette recipe

Ingredients Amount
Eggs, beaten 3
Sunflower oil 1 tsp
Butter 1 tsp
Hungry student 1 
Stir the eggs with a fork. Salt. 
Pour in milk. Stir the scrambled eggs. Prepare microwave-safe utensils. 
Pour the omelet mass into the dishes. 
Cook in the microwave on 1000 power for 1.5 minutes, 800 - 2 minutes, 600 - 4 minutes. 
End. You can feed the student.

Happy student
